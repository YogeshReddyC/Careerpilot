About CareerPilot

CareerPilot is an AI-powered resume analyzer. Paste your resume and a job description, and CareerPilot uses Google's Gemini AI to assess your overall fit, highlight your strengths, flag gaps, and suggest concrete improvements — in seconds.

How it works

  1. Log in and paste your resume into the Home screen
  2. Paste the job description you're targeting
  3. Click Analyze Fit
  4. Get a structured, AI-generated breakdown of your fit

Built with

  • FastAPI (Python) — backend
  • Vanilla HTML / CSS / JavaScript — frontend
  • Google Gemini API — the analysis engine
